Bio

Hello, my name is Joshua (June) Parsons, I'm a front end web developer, Designer, Computational Astrophysics student and back-end enthusiast. I'm well versed in web technologies such as bootstrap 4.0, CSS3 and HTML5. I have a growing portfolio on Github and I'm very active in Linked-in groups such as the Fluent design team and computational astrophysics. I have flexible experience in Python, Javascript, Java , C++ and C# with XAML including a fundamental knowledge of the universal windows platform with C#.

I am currently a first year student at York University taking Astrophysics. I strive to become a competent Computational Astrophysicist and later train to be an Astronaut. My passion for space and understanding the universe is the biggest aspect of my life and who I am. I hope to expand on this during my time in university both in my academic career and with outside opportunities .

While software is a huge part of my passion, I have a huge lust for hardware endeavors. I have experience in Arduino hardware hacks as well as scratch built electronics(such as a sumobot with hand built PCBs) In the future I'm hoping to get more acquainted with the raspberry pi ecosystem of hardware; and explore more complex hardware projects such as cubesat's and avionics hardware.

This site is both my portfolio and a sort of digital sandbox for future projects, designs, and ideas. If you would like to inquire as to what I could do for you or would like to team up on a project or just chat, feel free to browse the contact section!
